Before choosing earphones，Understanding these key factors：

  • Ear tipssize： Choose models with XS/S sizeEar tipsProduct，siliconeandfoam materials are softer
  • Earphone weight： Per earbud is best under 6g，Reduce ear canal pressure
  • Wearing Style： In-ear: go for smaller sizes，Open-ear/Bone conduction completely avoids ear canal issues
  • Over-ear： Go for lightweight models（<250g），Soft ear cushions，Adjustable headband
