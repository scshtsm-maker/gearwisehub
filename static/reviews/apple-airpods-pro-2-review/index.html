Quick Verdict

Rating: 4.3 / 5 — Best iPhone earbuds, but Android users should look elsewhere

Apple AirPods Pro 2 (USB-C) on Amazon ↗

The AirPods Pro 2 remains the best wireless earbud for iPhone users in 2026 — but not by the margin it once had. Competitors like the Soundcore Liberty 4 NC have closed the gap in ANC quality, while undercutting Apple's $249 price by $150+. Where the AirPods Pro 2 still wins decisively is ecosystem integration: the instant pairing, auto-switching between Apple devices, and spatial audio with head tracking create an experience no competitor can replicate.

Noise Cancellation

Apple's H2 chip processes noise cancellation 2x faster than the original H1, and it shows. The AirPods Pro 2 blocks roughly 90% of low-frequency ambient noise — subway rumble, airplane engines, HVAC hum. That's competitive with the Sony WH-1000XM5 in earbud form, which is remarkable.

The adaptive transparency mode is still the best in the business. It lets you hear your surroundings naturally while reducing dangerous sounds (sirens, construction) to safe levels. We tested this in NYC traffic and it works flawlessly — you hear conversations clearly but the jackhammer two blocks away is dialed down.

ANC modes: Off, Noise Cancellation, Transparency, Adaptive (new — auto-switches between ANC and transparency based on environment)

Sound Quality

The AirPods Pro 2 uses a custom Apple-designed driver and amplifier. The sound signature is slightly warm with clean mids and soft (not overwhelming) bass. It's a safe, pleasant tuning that works for everything from podcasts to pop to classical.

Audiophiles will want more. There's no custom EQ in iOS — only the basic "Tone Settings" slider. Compared to the Soundcore Liberty 4 NC with its 22 EQ presets, the AirPods Pro 2 feels limited. If you like to tune your sound, this is a frustration point.

Lossless audio: The USB-C model supports lossless audio with Apple Vision Pro at 20-bit/48kHz via a dedicated wireless protocol. With iPhone, you still get AAC 256kbps — no lossless. This is a minor upgrade that most users won't notice.

Spatial Audio & Features

Personalized Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking is the AirPods Pro 2's party trick. With supported content on Apple Music and Netflix, the soundstage wraps around you as you turn your head — like wearing a portable surround system. It's genuinely impressive with movies and spatial music tracks.

Other features:

  • Conversation Awareness: Lower volume and enhance voices when you start speaking
  • Swipe gesture on stem: Volume control without touching your phone
  • Find My with Precision Finding: U1 chip in case for exact location
  • Adaptive EQ: Automatically adjusts frequencies based on ear shape and fit

Battery Life

The AirPods Pro 2 delivers 6 hours with ANC on and 30 hours total with the charging case. That's solid but not class-leading — the Soundcore Liberty 4 NC gives you 10 hours per charge and 50 hours total.

  • Single charge (ANC on): 6 hours
  • Single charge (ANC off): 7 hours
  • Total with case: 30 hours
  • 5-minute quick charge: ~1 hour playback
  • Case charging: USB-C or MagSafe wireless

Comfort & Fit

At 5.3g per earbud, the AirPods Pro 2 is among the lightest earbuds available. The silicone tips come in four sizes (XS, S, M, L) — the extra XS size is new and helps users with smaller ear canals. The ear tip fit test in iOS confirms a proper seal.

In our 4-hour comfort test, the AirPods Pro 2 caused minimal ear fatigue. The vented design relieves pressure that some sealed earbuds create. This is an area where Apple genuinely excels — many competitors cause discomfort after 2+ hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are AirPods Pro 2 still worth buying in 2026?

Yes, if you're in the Apple ecosystem. The seamless iPhone/Mac/Apple Watch integration remains unmatched. However, if you use Android, there are better options for less money — see our Best Wireless Earbuds Under $100 guide.

What is the difference between AirPods Pro 2 Lightning and USB-C?

The USB-C model (2023+) adds dust resistance (IP54), lossless audio with Apple Vision Pro, and a lanyard loop on the case. ANC, sound quality, and battery life are identical between the two versions.

Do AirPods Pro 2 work with Android phones?

They connect via Bluetooth and play audio fine, but you lose spatial audio, auto-switching, Find My, battery optimization, and the custom EQ. An Android user should consider the Soundcore Liberty 4 NC instead — better ANC, better battery, and $150 less.

Should I upgrade from AirPods Pro 1 to Pro 2?

If your original AirPods Pro still work fine, the upgrade isn't urgent. The Pro 2 offers better ANC, longer battery, adaptive transparency, and volume swipe controls — nice improvements but not revolutionary. If your Pro 1 battery is degraded, then yes, the Pro 2 is a meaningful upgrade.
