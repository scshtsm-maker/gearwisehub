AirPods Pro 3 In-Depth Review

iPhone 用户的终极选择

Apple AirPods Pro 3 是 2026 年发布的最新旗舰真无线耳机，搭载全新 H3 芯片，在降噪深度、通透模式和空间音频方面全面超越前代。配合全新的泡沫耳塞设计，被动隔音效果显著提升，使整体降噪体验达到真无线耳机的新高度。

Noise Cancellation

The AirPods Pro 3 ANC system is powered by the H3 chip working with a redesigned acoustic chamber, delivering approximately 25% deeper noise cancellation compared to the AirPods Pro 2. In environments with constant low-frequency noise like subways and airplanes, the AirPods Pro 3 nearly eliminates background sound — the best ANC performance in the true wireless category.

The most impressive feature is the AirPods Pro 3's Transparency Mode. The H3 chip's processing power delivers remarkably natural ambient sound with virtually no electronic artifacts. When you need to have a conversation or stay aware of your surroundings, the transparency mode outperforms all competitors.

The new Adaptive Audio feature automatically adjusts ANC intensity based on ambient noise, balancing focus with awareness. Conversation Awareness detects when you start speaking and automatically lowers the volume while switching to transparency mode — ideal for office environments.

Sound Quality

The AirPods Pro 3 features a redesigned driver with deeper bass extension and wider dynamic range. Apple's computational audio technology enhances detail retrieval while maintaining the signature balanced Apple sound signature.

For Apple Music subscribers, the AirPods Pro 3 supports Spatial Audio and lossless audio (optimized via Bluetooth bandwidth), delivering an incredibly immersive experience when watching movies or listening to Spatial Audio tracks.

Battery and Charging

With ANC on, the AirPods Pro 3 delivers approximately 6 hours of earbud battery and up to 30 hours total with the case. It supports MagSafe wireless charging and Lightning wired charging (USB-C adapter also works), with 5 minutes of charging providing about 1 hour of playback.

Comfort and Fit

The AirPods Pro 3 debuts a new foam ear tip design that better fills the ear canal for improved passive isolation while maintaining comfort. Four sizes (XS/S/M/L) are included in the box, ensuring a proper fit for most users.

Pros and Cons

Pros:
  • Deepest active noise cancellation in the industry
  • Most natural transparency mode
  • Best-in-class Apple ecosystem integration
  • Comfortable foam ear tips
  • Smart features: Adaptive Audio, Conversation Awareness
Cons:
  • AAC only — no hi-res codec support
  • Limited experience on Android devices
  • Premium price point

Sony WF-1000XM6 In-Depth Review

The ANC Flagship with Hi-Fi Sound

The Sony WF-1000XM6 is Sony's latest flagship true wireless earbuds released in 2026, featuring the all-new QN3e processor and V2 chip for dual breakthroughs in noise cancellation and sound quality. With LDAC hi-res Bluetooth transmission and an 8.4mm Driver X driver, it ranks among the best-sounding true wireless ANC earbuds available.

Check Price on Amazon →

Noise Cancellation

The WF-1000XM6 ANC system uses a QN3e + V2 dual-chip setup with 6 microphones, delivering approximately 15% deeper noise cancellation than the XM5. Notably, the WF-1000XM6's ear pressure control eliminates the pressure sensation during extended wear — especially impressive during air travel.

The new atmospheric pressure balancing technology automatically adjusts the ANC curve based on cabin pressure, maintaining consistent noise cancellation during airplane takeoff and landing — a feature no other brand has matched yet.

Sound Quality

The WF-1000XM6's biggest strength is sound quality. LDAC hi-res Bluetooth (up to 990kbps/24bit) enables near Hi-Res wireless audio on supported Android devices. DSEE Extreme AI upscaling real-time restores high-frequency details lost in compressed audio.

In real listening tests, the WF-1000XM6 delivers a balanced three-band response: low frequencies are solid and punchy with excellent elasticity; mids place vocals front and center with nuanced emotion; highs are clean, transparent, and naturally extended. The wide soundstage and clear separation put it at the top of the true wireless class.

Battery and Charging

WF-1000XM6 在开启降噪的情况下，耳机本体续航约 8 小时，配合充电盒总续航约 24 小时。支持 USB-C 有线快充和 Qi 无线充电，充电 3 分钟可提供约 1 小时续航。

功能特性

The Sony Headphones Connect app offers extensive customization: 10-band adjustable EQ, Clear Bass boost, Adaptive Sound Control, 360 Reality Audio, and more. Multipoint connection lets you stay paired to two devices simultaneously.

Bose QC Ultra 2 In-Depth Review

The Gold Standard for Comfort

The Bose QuietComfort Ultra Earbuds 2 is Bose's latest flagship for 2026, building on the QC series' legendary comfortable fit while significantly improving noise cancellation depth and sound quality. If comfort is your top priority, the QC Ultra 2 is the clear winner.

Check Price on Amazon →

Noise Cancellation

The QC Ultra 2 carries Bose's latest ANC chip, matching the AirPods Pro 3 and WF-1000XM6 in noise cancellation depth. It excels at eliminating constant low-frequency noise like airplane engines and air conditioning hum — making it an excellent travel companion.

Bose's proprietary CustomTune technology automatically calibrates EQ and ANC curves to your ear shape, guaranteeing an optimized experience for every listener.

Sound Quality

The QC Ultra 2 delivers Bose's signature warm sound: well-controlled bass, full-bodied mids, and smooth, non-fatiguing highs. It supports aptX Adaptive hi-res codec (with compatible source devices), resulting in an easy-to-listen-to sound profile.

Bose's Immersive Audio creates a virtual surround soundstage, dramatically enhancing movie watching and gaming experiences.

Comfort and Fit

Comfort is the QC Ultra 2's biggest selling point. The Bose StayHear Max ear wings securely conform to your ear while remaining pressure-free during extended wear. Across two weeks of testing, the QC Ultra 2 remained comfortable even after 4+ hours of continuous use — the most comfortable fit of the three.

All three earbuds reach the ceiling of true wireless ANC performance, but each has distinct advantages in specific scenarios:

Scenario Best Choice Why

Air Travel Sony WF-1000XM6 Atmospheric pressure balancing, best ear pressure control
Office AirPods Pro 3 Most natural transparency, Conversation Awareness

Sound Quality Deep Dive

The three earbuds take different approaches to sound:

  • AirPods Pro 3: Natural and balanced, strong spatial audio immersion — ideal for Apple Music subscribers
  • Sony WF-1000XM6: Rich detail, wide soundstage, LDAC hi-fi quality — built for audiophiles
  • Bose QC Ultra 2: Warm and fatigue-free, full mids — great for pop music and long listening sessions

If you chase absolute sound quality, the Sony WF-1000XM6 is your pick. If you prefer a natural, balanced presentation, the AirPods Pro 3 won't disappoint. The Bose QC Ultra 2's warm character is better suited for relaxed, casual listening.

Buying Guide

Choose AirPods Pro 3 if:

  • You're an iPhone user
  • You want the best ANC depth and most natural transparency
  • You frequently use Apple Music and spatial audio
  • You value smart features like Conversation Awareness

Choose Sony WF-1000XM6 if:

  • You're an Android user or hi-fi enthusiast
  • You fly frequently (atmospheric pressure balancing is a game-changer)
  • You love tweaking EQ and customizing settings
  • You need multipoint pairing

Choose Bose QC Ultra 2 if:

  • Comfort is your absolute priority
  • You prefer a warm, fatigue-free sound signature
  • You wear earbuds for extended periods
  • You mostly use earbuds at a desk (battery life isn't critical)

Frequently Asked Questions

Should you upgrade from AirPods Pro 2 to AirPods Pro 3?

Upgrading from the AirPods Pro 2 to the Pro 3 gets you deeper ANC, more natural transparency, foam tips, and Adaptive Audio. If your budget allows, it's a worthwhile upgrade. If your Pro 2 still serves you well, keep it.

What's the difference between the WF-1000XM6 and WF-1000XM5?

The WF-1000XM6 uses a new QN3e processor, delivering ~15% deeper ANC, a new atmospheric pressure balancing feature, and better sound detail. It's also more rounded for a better fit with superior ear pressure control. Upgrading is worthwhile if you're an audiophile or frequent flyer.

Which of the three is best for sports?

Bose QC Ultra 2 的 StayHear Max 耳翼设计在运动中稳固性最佳，佩戴舒适度高。AirPods Pro 3 的 IP54 防水等级最高。Sony WF-1000XM6 Relatively larger form factor，不太适合剧烈运动。
