FAQ

Which has better noise canceling: over-ear headphones or true wireless earbuds?？

Over-ear headphones usually have better noise canceling than true wireless earbuds，Because over-ear headphones have larger ear cups that provide better passive isolation，At the same time, the larger space can accommodate stronger ANC chips and larger batteries。If you pursue ultimate noise canceling performance，Over-ear headphones are a better choice。

AirPods Max 2 Worth buying?？

If you are iPhone Users，Pursue the best ecosystem integration and spatial audio experience，AirPods Max 2 Is worth it。But if you are price-sensitive or need to wear them for long periods，Sony or Bose Might be a better choice。

Do over-ear headphones get hot when worn in summer?？

Wearing over-ear headphones in summer does feel stuffy，Especially the ear cushion part。AirPods Max 2 's mesh canopy design can somewhat alleviate the stuffy feeling on top of the head，But the ear cushion part will still accumulate heat。If frequently used outdoors in summer，Recommend choosing ear cushion materials with better breathability or considering true wireless earbuds。
