Design and Comfort

Blade-Style Stem Design

Samsung introduced a striking new blade-style stem with the Buds3 Pro, setting them apart visually from the stemless Buds2 Pro. The illuminated blade strips on each earbud add a distinctive aesthetic touch and serve as a functional indicator for status. The stem grip lets you adjust the fit with precision, and it doubles as a touch control surface.

At 5.4g per earbud, the Buds3 Pro is lightweight and comfortable for extended listening sessions. The IPX7 rating is a notable upgrade — you can submerge them in up to 1 meter of water for 30 minutes, making them genuinely workout-ready and rain-proof.

Sound Quality

The dual-driver setup — an 11mm dynamic driver paired with a 6.5mm planar tweeter — delivers a wide soundstage with impressive detail. The planar tweeter handles high frequencies with exceptional clarity, while the dynamic driver delivers punchy, controlled bass. Together they produce a balanced, audiophile-friendly sound signature that works across genres.

With 24-bit / 96kHz audio support via Samsung Scalable Codec (on Samsung devices), you get studio-grade wireless audio quality. The difference is most noticeable in high-resolution tracks from services like Tidal, Apple Music, or Amazon Music Ultra HD.

360 Audio with head tracking creates an immersive spatial sound experience, particularly effective for Dolby Atmos content. The head tracking feature keeps the soundstage anchored to your device even as you turn your head — a subtle but compelling effect for movies and music videos.

Noise Cancelling

Samsung fitted the Buds3 Pro with a dedicated ANC chip that works alongside the dual-driver system for superior noise cancellation. Low-frequency rumble — airplane engines, subway noise, HVAC hum — is reduced dramatically. Mid-range sounds like office chatter and traffic are also attenuated effectively.

The Voice Detect feature automatically switches to ambient sound mode when you start speaking, letting you have a quick conversation without removing the earbuds. It resumes ANC after a few seconds of silence.

Call quality is excellent. Samsung deep learning-based noise reduction separates your voice from background noise with impressive accuracy, making phone and video calls crystal clear even in noisy environments.

Galaxy AI Features

The headline feature of the Buds3 Pro is Galaxy AI integration. On a Samsung Galaxy phone, you get:

  • Live Translate: Real-time voice translation during phone calls with support for dozens of languages. Your conversation partner can speak in their language while you hear a real-time translation in your ears.
  • Interpreter: Use your Buds3 Pro as a translation earpiece in face-to-face conversations — you hear the translation, they hear it through your phone speaker.
  • AI Voice Compose: Use voice commands to compose messages with AI-generated responses tailored to context.

These features require a Samsung Galaxy phone (Android 8 or later with Galaxy AI). On other devices, you get all the core audio and ANC features but miss out on the AI capabilities.

Battery Life

The Galaxy Buds3 Pro delivers 7 hours of playback with ANC enabled on a single charge — a meaningful step up from the 5 hours of the Buds2 Pro. The charging case adds 23 hours for a total of 30 hours.

A 5-minute charge via USB-C gives you approximately 1 hour of playback. The case also supports wireless charging via Qi and Samsung PowerShare, allowing you to top up from your Galaxy phone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Galaxy Buds3 Pro different from the Buds2 Pro?

The Galaxy Buds3 Pro features Galaxy AI integration for real-time translation and interpreter features, enhanced 24-bit audio with an upgraded dual-driver system, improved ANC with a dedicated chip, and a new blade-style design with illuminated strips. Battery life is also extended to 7 hours with ANC on, up from 5 hours.

Does Galaxy Buds3 Pro work well with iPhones?

Yes. Galaxy Buds3 Pro work with any Bluetooth device. On iPhone you get AAC codec, standard touch controls, and all audio features including ANC and spatial audio. You lose Galaxy AI features (Live Translate, Interpreter) which require a Galaxy phone.

Is the Galaxy Buds3 Pro comfortable to wear?

Yes. The Buds3 Pro uses a new blade-style stem design that distributes weight differently from standard buds. The ear tips create a good seal and the earbuds stay put during movement. Samsung includes three ear tip sizes.

Does Galaxy Buds3 Pro support LDAC?

No. Galaxy Buds3 Pro supports SBC, AAC, and Samsung proprietary Scalable Codec. The Scalable Codec delivers excellent quality on Samsung devices, but there is no LDAC support.

Can I use Galaxy Buds3 Pro for workouts?

Absolutely. With an IPX7 rating, the Buds3 Pro can handle sweat, rain, and even brief submersion in water. The secure fit from the blade-style design also helps them stay in place during exercise.
