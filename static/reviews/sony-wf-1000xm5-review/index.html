Design and Comfort

The Sony WF-1000XM5 continues the understated premium design language of the XM series while making notable refinements. Compared to the XM4, the earbuds are approximately 25% smaller and the weight drops from 7.3g to 5.9g. The more rounded contours sit more naturally in the ear, reducing the protruding appearance common in traditional wireless earbuds.

Sony equips the WF-1000XM5 with newly developed polyurethane foam ear tips. These tips are soft and elastic, effectively filling the ear canal for better physical isolation while maintaining excellent comfort. The packaging includes four sizes (SS/S/M/L) so users can find the ideal fit.

After a week of daily commuting tests (about 2 hours of wear per day), the earbuds stayed firmly in place during light jogging and mild exercise. The IPX4 water resistance rating is sufficient for workout sweat and light rain; the case also carries an IPX2 rating for added peace of mind outdoors.

The charging case has a matte finish with smooth lid action, operable with one hand. A built-in hall sensor auto-connects when the earbuds are removed and disconnects when returned. Both black and platinum silver colorways are versatile across different use cases.

Sound Quality

The WF-1000XM5 features Sony's purpose-built 8.4mm Dynamic Driver X paired with the V2 integrated processor, achieving a significant breakthrough in acoustic design. Compared to the XM4's 6mm driver, the new unit delivers deeper bass extension, wider dynamic range, and soundstage layering closer to high-end over-ear headphones.

For Bluetooth codecs, the WF-1000XM5 supports LDAC (up to 990kbps/24bit), AAC, and SBC. LDAC is currently the highest standard for Bluetooth audio transmission on Android devices. On LDAC-capable devices (Sony players, flagship Android phones), you can unlock near Hi-Res wireless audio quality.

DSEE Extreme (Digital Sound Enhancement Engine Extreme) is Sony's exclusive AI upscaling technology that analyzes and restores high-frequency details lost in compressed audio in real time. Even when streaming Spotify or Apple Music over AAC or SBC, DSEE Extreme noticeably improves the clarity and realism of the music.

In real listening tests (LDAC connected, Spotify Ultimate quality), the WF-1000XM5 delivers well-balanced sound across all three frequencies: Bass is tight and punchy, with electronic kick drums and bass guitars sounding impactful; Mids are centered with nuanced vocal emotion; Highs are clean and natural on strings and female vocals. The soundstage is wide laterally with clear depth layers -- overall one of the top performers among true wireless earbuds.

The Sony Headphones Connect App also supports customizable EQ with Clear Bass, 10 adjustable frequency bands, and multiple presets. For experienced audiophiles, deep sound customization is a major bonus.

Active Noise Cancellation

The WF-1000XM5's ANC system is built around 6 microphones (3 feedforward + 3 feedback on each side) working in tandem with the V2 integrated processor -- Sony's most powerful true wireless noise-canceling solution to date. Compared to the XM4, noise cancellation depth improves by approximately 20%, with particularly strong performance in the low-to-mid frequency range (20Hz-1kHz).

In head-to-head testing against key competitors:

  • vs. Bose QuietComfort Ultra Earbuds: ANC levels are comparable -- both at the top of the industry. Bose edges out slightly on high-frequency noise (voices, keyboard clicks), while Sony performs better on sustained low-frequency noise (airplane engines, subway rumble).
  • vs. Apple AirPods Pro 2: Sony leads by about 2-3dB in low-to-mid frequency ANC depth, with a clear advantage in loud subway cars and airplane cabins. AirPods Pro 2's adaptive transparency mode and conversation detection are more tailored to daily use scenarios.

The WF-1000XM5 also features Auto NC Optimizer, which analyzes ear shape, fit position, and ambient air pressure to automatically calibrate the ANC curve -- ensuring optimal performance even in special environments like airplane travel. Combined with foam tip isolation, the overall noise-canceling experience is nearly unmatched in true wireless earbuds.

Battery and Charging

Battery life on the Sony WF-1000XM5 is satisfying. With ANC on, the earbuds deliver approximately 8 hours of continuous playback; with the oval charging case, total battery reaches about 24 hours -- enough to cover two to three days of daily commuting or a long international flight.

With ANC off, earbud battery extends to about 12 hours and total to approximately 36 hours, consistent with Sony's official figures.

Charging options include:

  • USB-C fast charge: 3 minutes delivers approximately 1 hour of playback (~2.5 hours to fully charge earbuds)
  • Qi wireless charging: Compatible with most Qi-standard wireless charging pads (~3.5 hours to full charge)
  • Case reverse wireless sharing: The case can reverse-charge other Qi-capable devices (e.g., phones) in emergencies

The case bottom has a USB-C port and LED indicator: green (>80%), yellow (20-80%), red (<20%). The Sony Headphones Connect App also shows precise battery percentages for both earbuds and case.

Features

The WF-1000XM5 inherits Sony's extensive library of premium headphone features, offering extensive practical features:

  • Multipoint Connection: Connect two Bluetooth devices simultaneously (e.g., phone + laptop) with automatic seamless switching for calls and music -- no manual disconnecting needed. A major convenience for multi-device users.
  • Speak-to-Chat: Automatically pauses music and activates ambient sound when you start talking, then resumes playback after approximately 15-30 seconds (customizable). No earbud touching required.
  • Head Gesture Control: Nod or shake your head to answer or reject calls -- extremely useful when your hands are occupied.
  • Google Fast Pair / Swift Pair: Auto-popup pairing prompts when near Android phones or Windows PCs for instant pairing.
  • 360 Reality Audio: Sony's immersive spatial audio format, available with supporting streaming services (Tidal, Deezer).
  • Adaptive Sound Control: Automatically switches ANC/transparency modes based on your activity (still, walking, running, commuting).
  • Voice assistant support: Built-in support for Alexa / Google Assistant for music control and information queries.
  • DSEE Extreme + Clear Bass: AI upscaling and bass enhancement that noticeably improves compressed audio playback quality.
  • Find Your Equalizer: App-based hearing test that auto-optimizes EQ curves based on your personal hearing profile.

FAQ

How good is the Sony WF-1000XM5 noise cancellation?

The WF-1000XM5 features a 6-microphone ANC system and V2 integrated processor, making it one of the strongest noise-canceling true wireless earbuds available. In low-frequency noise environments (airplane engines, AC hum), ANC approaches the level of some over-ear headphones, with overall noise reduction approximately 20% deeper than the XM4.

How does the Sony WF-1000XM5 sound?

The WF-1000XM5 is equipped with an 8.4mm Dynamic Driver X and supports LDAC hi-res Bluetooth codec (up to 990kbps) and DSEE Extreme audio enhancement. Midrange and high-frequency resolution is outstanding with a wide soundstage. Sound quality is top-tier among true wireless earbuds, approaching some wired Hi-Fi headphones.

How long does the WF-1000XM5 battery last?

With ANC on, the earbuds deliver approximately 8 hours of continuous playback; with the charging case, total battery is about 24 hours. With ANC off: approximately 12 hours (earbuds) + 24 hours (total). Supports USB-C fast charge (3 min charge = 1 hour playback) and Qi wireless charging.

Are the Sony WF-1000XM5 good for working out?

The WF-1000XM5 carries an IPX4 rating for water and sweat resistance, standing up to splashes and workout sweat -- suitable for gym sessions and outdoor jogging. Submerging in water or swimming is not recommended. The included multi-size memory foam tips provide a secure, comfortable fit for most users.

Sony WF-1000XM5 vs. AirPods Pro 2: Which is better?

Both are close in positioning with distinct advantages. The WF-1000XM5 wins on sound (LDAC, higher resolution), ANC depth, and custom EQ personalization -- ideal for Android users and those with high audio demands. AirPods Pro 2 integrates deeply with the Apple ecosystem (Spatial Audio, auto-switching, Siri) and is the better choice for iPhone users. Your primary device and ecosystem preference should be the deciding factor.
